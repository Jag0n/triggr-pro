## NRAI Competition Tiers (Pistol & Rifle)

| Competition | Level | Who Can Participate | Key Qualifier |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sub-Youth / Inter-School (KSS) | Entry-level | School-age shooters | State selection |
| Zonal Championship | Regional | State affiliated shooters | MQS from State Championship |
| All India GV Mavlankar (AIGVMSC) | National open | All NRAI-affiliated | MQS from State/Zonal |
| National Shooting Championship (NSCC) | National elite | Top performers | MQS from AIGVMSC/Zonal |
| India Open / Selection Trials | National selection | NSCC qualifiers | NRAI selection criteria |
| SAI / Railway / Services Championship | Institutional | SAI/Armed forces affiliated | Institutional eligibility |
